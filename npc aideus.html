AIDeus Venture Studio

Venture Capital at the Edge of Innovation

Aideus is the venture capital arm of Norway Propco. We back bold ideas and visionary founders building the future of sustainable living, design, and intelligent infrastructure. Rooted in Scandinavian values, we focus on early-stage investments with long-term partnership in mind.
